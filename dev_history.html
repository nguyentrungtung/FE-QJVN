CHARMAN PROFILE

  • 2001

    Since 2001, Mr. Shu Yu Hui made in-depth research on the popularization, professionalization and systematization of the natural medicine; and he deeply realized that even the better culturecan’t be popularized if there is no advanced operation mode and modern science and technology. Therefore, according to the accumulated marketing strategy experience in many years, he cooperated with several professors from Tsinghua University to devote to the popularization of the natural medicine.

  • 2004

    In 2004, Mr. Shu Yu Hui established QuanJian Natural Medicine Science and Technology Development Co., Ltd inTianjin. The company takes “carrying forward Chinese natural medicine culture and promoting the national health plan project” as the tenet, takes “deducing the natural medicine and sharing the natural longevity together” as the corporate management philosophy, and dedicates in the popularization and promotion of the natural medicine based on establishing the health service centers of natural medicine at home and.
